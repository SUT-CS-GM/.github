# Generative Models (Fall 2025)

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for the Generative Models course (Fall 2025 semester) at Sharif University of Technology.

* **University:** Sharif University of Technology (SUT)
* **Department:** Department of Mathematical Sciences
* **Group:** Computer Science (CS)
* **Organization Handle:** `SUT-CS-GM`

---

## About This Course

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the principles, algorithms, and applications of generative modeling. We will explore a wide variety of models and techniques, from foundational probabilistic models to large-scale generative AI.

## Purpose of This Organization

This organization hosts all official course materials, codebases, and project repositories. You will find:

* The official **course website**
* Lecture slides and supplementary notes
* Material for **homework assignments**
